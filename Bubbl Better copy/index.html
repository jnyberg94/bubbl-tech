Saving the World's Oldest Forests—one tree at a time

We're a venture capital firm backed by Nvidia and Rivian funding innovative tech to prevent the destruction of the natural world

Our Mission

Bubbl was created for one reason: give innovators the tools to make the world a more sustainable place. Our funding has already resulted in products like Synthetic Wood and made possible the restoration of major ecosystems across 4 continents.

Notable Projects

Aerial Reseeding

Using a fleet of drones with LiDAR capabilities, we can accurately disperse thousands of seeds per second with pin point precision.

Forest Fire Prediction

Using predictive modelling, and AI information systems, we can determine how a forest fire is likely to spread, to dispatch the most effective solution.

Synthetic Wood

A mixture of modified corn, chitin, and polymer called ‘Resol’ are combined to create a more ecofriendly, and easier to produce wood replacement.
